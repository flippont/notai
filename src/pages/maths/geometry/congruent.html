Congruent triangles are two completely identical triangles that fit perfectly inside of one another. If a triangle is congruent, then they are also similar.

There are four tests for congruency.

  1. SSS – When all three corresponding sides are equal in length (Side Side Side)
  2. SAS – When two corresponding sides are equal, and the corresponding angle between them are equal. (Side Angle Side)
  3. ASA – When two corresponding angles are equal, and another corresponding side is equal. (Angle Side Angle)
  4. HS – A pair of hypotenuses and another pair of corresponding sides are equal in two right angled triangles (Hypotenuse Side)
