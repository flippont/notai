400BC

Democritus proposed that matter was made up of indivisible particles, called “atomos” (Greek word for atom)

1808

John Dalton put forward an atomic theory: All atoms are indivisible, and all matter is made up of atoms.

1875

William Crookes discovered Cathode Rays, which are radiation rays that travel in a straight line that possess energy

1897

JJ Thomson discovered negatively charged particles called electrons. He also calculated the ratio of the charge of an electron to its mass.

1909

Robert Millikan tried to determine the charge and mass of an electron using the oil drop experiment

1909

Ernest Rutherford discovered the nucleus by bombarding a piece of gold foil with alpha particles

1909

James Chadwick The discovery of the neutron . He found a radiation consisting of no charge
