One syllable verbs

For the one syllable future tense verbs, you use faidh/fidh as the endings. For the we part, you use faimid/fimid. For the negative statement, you use ní + séimhiú, and for the question you use an an + urú
For the saorbhriathar, use far/fear, as endings.

Two syllable verbs

For the two syllable verbs, you remove the endings like in the present tense, and then you use óimid/eoimid as the endings.
For the saorbhriathar, you use ófar/eofar as the endings.

Irregular Verbs

There are only 11 Irish Irregular verbs, which are the same for all four tenses.
