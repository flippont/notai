The only numbers that use “et” are the numbers which end in 1 (under 100) 

E.g. Vingt et un 

Deux mille huit soxiante et un

Some numbers take an “S” at the end 

  1. Multiples of 100 
    E.g. Deux cents /Sept cents 
  1. The s is dropped when a number is added onto a number in the hundreds 
    E.g. Sept cents mais sept cent vingt trois.  
  1. The number 80 
    E.g. quatre vingts (but not 81-89)
